Como construir uma casa

Yukie is the recent and latest addition to the Four Devas, a group made of the strongest fighters in the world. This is a fact that is relatively unknown to the public however, due to Yukie's withdrawn personality.

  • Tamanho do terreno: 302 m/2
  • Área a ser construída: Aprox 120 m/2
  • Padrão para construção: Estilo Kawakami

Yukie is a fighter of most special interest to Momoyo Kawakami due to her strength and potential with Momoyo watching for her development with a desire to face Yukie in a decent fight. Yukie represents Respect (礼, Rei), one of the seven virtues of martial arts.
