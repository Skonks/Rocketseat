About me

Yukie Mayuzumi is one of the main heroines in Maji De Watashi Ni Koi Shinasai! anime and manga. She is a newcomer to the Kazama Family who has the ambition to make one hundred friends, the daughter of Taisei Mayuzumi and one of the love interests to Yamato Naoe in Majikoi! and Majikoi! S.

História

Being trained constantly in sword fighting and martial arts from a young age, Yukie failed to develop any real social skills and was unable to make friends with other kids. She has since become a really shy and awkward girl, and she unintentionally scares off many people with her weird behavior and her scary smiles.

Trabalhos

Yukie is one of The Big Four or 4 Devas, getting the title by defeating Takae Tachibana, stripping her of the title. The complete name of her title is Yukie Mayuzumi of the West.
