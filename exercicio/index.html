Yukie Mayuzumi

Yukie Mayuzumi (黛 由紀江) is one of the main heroines in Maji De Watashi Ni Koi Shinasai! anime and manga. She is a newcomer to the Kazama Family who has the ambition to make one hundred friends, the daughter of Taisei Mayuzumi and one of the love interests to Yamato Naoe in Majikoi! and Majikoi! S. Yukie is the recent and latest addition to the Four Devas, a group made of the strongest fighters in the world. This is a fact that is relatively unknown to the public however, due to Yukie's withdrawn personality


Christiane Friedrich

Christiane Friedrich (クリスティアーネ・フリードリヒ, Kurisutiāne Furīdorihi), also known as Chris (クリス Kurisu), is one of the main heroines in Maji De Watashi Ni Koi Shinasai!. She is a main character in the anime and the manga that is being published by Ace Comp Ace and one Yamato Naoe's love interests in Majikoi!. Chris is also one of the members of the Kazama Family and the sole member of the group from Germany as a transfer student of Kawakami Academy. She loves Japanese culture and the reason she came to Japan is to recognize her interest towards Japanese culture and things related to Japan. Unsurprisingly, Chris's familiarity with Japanese history as well as the military background of her family has also made her a decent strategist and a natural leader when called for.
